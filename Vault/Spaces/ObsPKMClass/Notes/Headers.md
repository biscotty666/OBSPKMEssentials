## Headers

To create a header, enter from 1-6 `#` signs followed by a space. Headers provide
- Visual structure
- Provides hierarchy and nesting
- Can be referenced directly in backlinks
- Auto-generated tables of contents
- Auto-generated folds

Example:

`###### Header 6`

displays

###### Header 6
